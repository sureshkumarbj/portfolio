Mission
To become the largest manufacturer of conveyor idlers and pulleys in India.
        To be the one stop source for all specialized Idler and pulley needs.
        To increase the turnover from the present levels to Rs. 1400 Millions($ 30 million) by 2012.
        To become a global player by 2012.
        To develop a keen sense of protecting the environment among employees and also achieve equitable growth of everyone at Kali.
 
  Profile
        Started operation in 1976 under the name and style of “Kali Material Handling Systems”.
        Another Company “Kali MHS Private Ltd” was started in the year 1983
        A branch unit was started in the year 1992 in the neighbouring Pondicherry state
        Corporatized in the year 2007 under the present name and style
        Entered into alliance with Conveyor and Piping systems, Australia in 2006
        Alliance with Intertrade – Ptolemaida for Greece market since 2007.
        New state of art plant started in 4 acre plot with covered shed of 35000 Sq. feet commissioned on March 7th 2009.
